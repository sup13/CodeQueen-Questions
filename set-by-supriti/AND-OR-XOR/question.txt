Given an integer N, find out :-
(i) xor of its divisors
(ii) or of its divisors
(iii) and of its divisors

Input :- The first line consists of an integer T, the number of testcases. T lines follow, each consisting of an integer N.

Output :- N lines, each line consisting of (i),(ii) and (iii) separated by a space.

Constraints:-
T<=1000000
1<=N<=1000000

Sample Input :-
5
3
10
16
20
25

Sample Output :-
2 3 1
12 15 0
31 31 0
28 31 0
29 29 1
